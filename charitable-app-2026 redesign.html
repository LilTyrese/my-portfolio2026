About this charity

For nearly 40 years, Food Bank For New York City has worked to end food poverty by organizing food, information, and support for community survival, empowerment, and dignity. The Food Bank works throughout the five boroughs providing food to over 1.5 million New Yorkers through a network of more than 1,000 community partners.
